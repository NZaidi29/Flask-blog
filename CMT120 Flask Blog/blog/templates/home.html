Welcome to my blog assessment 😎

Hello, and welcome to my blog. This blog was created as part of a university assessment. It was created using Flask, MySQL, HTML and CSS. For this reason it meets certain assessment requirements but does not have every feature I would want to implement for a regular blog, I do intend to work on it after this assignment to add more features and functionality.

You will find to the right there is a selection of featured blog posts, please feel free to read them. If you are looking for any particular topic feel free to search for a keyword in the search bar. You may also register for an account, save or like posts and leave comments. 😄

Working on this project was an interesting experience and has definitely made me appreciate the work thats goes into sites like Facebook or Youtube with so much dynamic content and also with such massive databases. I feel like I learned a lot during the process of making this and hopefully can do more interesting projects like this both on my own and through my course.
